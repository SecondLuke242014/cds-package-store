1. COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

CDS package store is to publish assets, but not just kids!

According to the United States law, children under 13 will not get their information collected; THE MODERATOR WILL DELETE THEIR ASSETS!

If you are aged 9-12 you will have parent supervision.

2. VIRUSES IN ASSETS

In the marketplace, their assets should be scanned for approval. If a virus is found, their asset is deleted. So if an unscanned asset gets seen in the marketplace, the developer will download the asset right away and put it in roblox studio. WITHOUT A WARNING ROBLOX WILL WARN/BAN/TERMINATE THE ACCOUNT!
